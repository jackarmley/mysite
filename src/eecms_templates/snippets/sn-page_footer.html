Colophon

Headings are set in Ostrich Sans, from the League of Movable Type. Body copy is Georgia (as it was more readable than Podkova). UI icons are from Snowcone, a scalable @font-face icon set developed by myself and the lovely folks at Tangent Snowball. Responsive videos work a treat thanks to FitVids.JS, by Chris Coyier and Paravel. The flexible grid system is inspired by an article by the great Elliot Jay Stocks, and the site is powered by Expression Engine. Curious? Find the site on Github.
