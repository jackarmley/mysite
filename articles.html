Oh Christmas tree…

I love Instagram; It doesn't feel as formalized as Flickr but feels more like a book to keep quick snaps of what you find whilst out and about. I took advantage of this instantanious-ness - and the fact that you can tag images to make collections - to amass a collection of Christmas tree pictures.
