Colophon

Headings are set in Ostrich Sans, from the League of Movable Type. Body copy is Podkova, from Google Fonts. UI icons are from Snowcone, a scalable @font-face icon set developed by myself and the lovely folks at Tangent Snowball. The flexible grid system is inspired by an article by the great Elliot Jay Stocks, and the site is powered by Expression Engine.
